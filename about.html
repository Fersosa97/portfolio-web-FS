Camino de Los Pioneros

Inaugurated in 2007, this place honors the pioneers of the city and consists of a 3.4 km path through a natural environment of about 30 hectares

Cerro El Centinela

This place is 6 km from the city. There you can walk through a forest, eat typical foods of Tandil and ride in the chairlift

Las Cavas

This is the result of the mining on the hills of the two lions, which together with subsequent rains gave rise to this magnificent natural beauty.

El Dique

Created after one of the most important inundations which Tandil suffered in 1951. Nowadays, there you can do a lot of sports such as walking, cycling, and nautic activities.

La Movediza

A big rock which has been famous for being kept balanced on the border of a hill until 1912, which it fell. Since 2007 a replica replace it

El Calvario

Inaugurated in 1943, it's the third most famous worldwide. Along the road, you can see all the life of Jesus until his death.

The Waterfall

Located at 6 km of Tandil center. It's an unadulterated and quiet place, where after raining you can see how the water is falling through the hills simulating a cascade.
